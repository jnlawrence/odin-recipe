Fried Chicken

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Make the breading.
  2. Dredge the Chicken.
  3. Fry the Chicken.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
